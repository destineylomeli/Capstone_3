# Capstone 3 - Analysis with Power BI

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes bookstore sales performance for the **South Region**, focusing on the states of **Florida, South Carolina, and Texas**. The report provides insights into sales trends, product categories, state performance, top selling books, shipping costs, and additional sales performance insights.

## Objectives
- Analyze bookstore sales across the South Region.
- Identify sales trends over time.
- Compare sales performance by category.
- Show the distributiion of sales by state.
- Highlight the highest selling books and authors (excluding textbooks).
- Estimate shipping costs and discounts.
- Add interactive features such as slicers, tooltips, and mobile layout.

## Report Pages

### Page 1 - Sales Overview
This page includes:
- Sales trend over time (Line Chart)
- Sales by Category (Clustered Column Chart)
- Sales by State (Donut Chart)
- Total Regional Sales (Card)
- State Slicer (Florida, South Carolina, Texas)

### Page 2 - Regional Book Analysis
This page includes:
- Top selling books in the South Region
- Author names with total sales
- Top 10 Authors by sales (Bar Chart)
- Total Book Sales (Card)
- State Slicer for interactive filtering

### Page 3 - Bonus: Shipping Cost Analysis
Includes:
- Estimated shipping cost
- Shipping discount
- Final Shipping cost
- Shipping cost by state
- Shipping cost KPI and target comparison

### Page 4 - Bonus: Sales Performance Insights
Includes:
- Top 10 best selling products
- Lowest performing products
- Top categories by sales
- Total South Region sales card
- State slicer

### Bonus Features
- Custom product tooltip showing sales and shipping details
- Mobile layout for report accessibility
- Interactive slicers for state level filtering

## Key Insights
- Technology & Accessories generated the highest sales.
- Florida contributed the largest share of sales within the South Region.
- Sales increased over the reporting period.
- Several authors consistently generated the highest revenue across the region.
